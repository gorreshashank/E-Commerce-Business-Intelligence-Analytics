# E-Commerce Business Intelligence & Customer Analytics

## Overview

This project analyzes retail sales transactions using MySQL and Power BI to uncover customer behavior, product performance, profitability drivers, and regional sales trends. The objective is to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making.

## Tech Stack

* MySQL
* Power BI
* Excel
* GitHub

## Project Workflow

Raw Sales Data
↓
Data Cleaning
↓
SQL Analysis
↓
Business Insights
↓
Power BI Dashboard
↓
Business Recommendations

## Business Questions Answered

### Customer Analytics

* Who are the top revenue-generating customers?
* Which customers generate high revenue but low profit?
* What is the Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)?
* Which customers place the most orders?

### Product Analytics

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* Which products generate the highest profit?
* Which products are loss-making?
* Which products lead their respective categories?

### Regional Analytics

* Which regions are most profitable?
* Which states generate the highest profit?
* Which states are loss-making?

## Key Insights

* Revenue is concentrated among a small group of high-value customers.
* Customer retention has a direct impact on business growth.
* A limited number of products generate most revenue and profit.
* Certain products and states operate at a loss.
* Regional profitability varies significantly across markets.
